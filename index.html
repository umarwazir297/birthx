About Us

 

BirthX is an app that allows birthing individuals to review their childbirth experience in regards to the care provided by their Obstetrician or Midwife. Using crowd-sourced reviews, it will be as a resource for pregnant individuals who are searching for a health care provider that fits their unique needs in curating the type of birth experience they desire. BirthX will also include numerous educational resources including but not limiting to: an explanation of the American Hospital Associations Bill of Patient Rights, suggested questions to ask when interviewing health care providers, the Bishop score and when to use it, and the risks and benefits of the most common medical interventions. Together, we can shift Maternal Health and Childbirth from being provider-center to woman or birthing individual centered and end obstetric violence and mortality.

Why Choose us
